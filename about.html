Our Story

Hello, I'm Santi Maya Ghimiray

Welcome to CeraHome, where passion for home decor meets Bhutanese craftsmanship. As a new online entrepreneur from Phuentsholing, Bhutan, I've always been fascinated by how spaces can tell stories and evoke emotions.

My journey began with a deep appreciation for our rich cultural heritage and a desire to share it with the world through beautiful, functional home decor items. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected or handcrafted to bring warmth and character to your living spaces.

Music is my constant inspiration - the rhythm, harmony, and emotions in melodies often translate into the designs and arrangements you'll find at CeraHome. Just as music can transform a mood, the right decor can completely change the feel of a room.

At CeraHome, we believe your home should be a reflection of your personality and values. Whether you're looking for traditional Bhutanese pieces or modern designs with cultural influences, we have something special for every home.

Our Mission

To bring the beauty of Bhutanese craftsmanship to homes worldwide, creating spaces that inspire, comfort, and tell unique stories through carefully curated decor pieces.
