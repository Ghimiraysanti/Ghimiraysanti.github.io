About Us

Our Story

Welcome to The Bhat Spot, where tradition meets taste! Founded by Santi Ghimiray, a passionate food enthusiast from Phuentsholing, Bhutan, our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you the most authentic Bhutanese thali experience.

Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Bhutan and Nepal, a love for bringing people together through food, Santi created The Bhat Spot to share the flavors of home with everyone.

Every dish is prepared with love, using traditional recipes and the freshest local ingredients to ensure an unforgettable dining experience.

Santi Ghimiray
Founder & Chef

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM every day, including weekends and holidays.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, we have several vegetarian options including our popular Vegetarian Delight Thali and various vegetable curries.

What is your delivery area?

We deliver within Phuentsholing town limits. Delivery charges may apply depending on distance.

How spicy is your food?

Traditional Bhutanese food is quite spicy, but we can adjust the spice level to your preference. Just let us know when ordering.

Do you accept credit cards?

Yes, we accept all major credit cards, as well as cash and mobile payments.
